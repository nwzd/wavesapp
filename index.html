How it works

Three steps to a real connection

No profile-browsing strangers from across the world. Wave & Vibe only works when you're actually there, in person.

📡

People nearby appear

Wave & Vibe scans using Bluetooth. People physically around you who also have Wave & Vibe open appear in your Nearby list. Keep both Bluetooth and Wi-Fi on — no internet connection needed, just the radios.

👋

You wave

Spot someone interesting? Tap Wave. They get notified with your name. If they wave back — it's mutual. No awkward one-sided gestures.

✦

You vibe — and connect

A mutual wave creates a Vibe. You both see each other's contact info (Instagram, email…) exactly as you set it up. The rest is up to you.

Trust & safety

Built to keep everyone safe

Wave & Vibe is for adults who want to make genuine connections. We take a few simple steps to make sure it stays that way.

🛡️

Children cannot use this app

Wave & Vibe requires users to be 18 or older — not because the app contains adult content, but because we are serious about protecting children. Anyone under 18 is blocked from creating a profile.

🤖

On-device photo check

When you upload a profile photo, Google ML Kit checks it entirely on your device — one face only, no inappropriate content. The photo never leaves your phone for this check.

🚫

Block & restrict

Block anyone instantly. Blocked users can no longer discover you. Repeated reports from multiple users trigger automatic restrictions — protecting the whole community.

Privacy first

Zero servers.
Your data stays
on your phone.

Everything — your profile, your connections, your contact info — lives only on your device. We have nothing to delete because we store nothing.

🔒

No cloud storage

Profiles are exchanged directly phone‑to‑phone over Bluetooth and Wi-Fi Direct — no relay, no server.

👁️

Contact only after mutual wave

Your Instagram, email, or other contact info is never visible until both parties wave.

🗑️

Delete = uninstall

Uninstalling the app removes everything. No deletion request needed, no residual data.

🔑

Device-local identity

Your Wave & Vibe ID is generated once on your device and never sent to any server. It is not linked to your real identity anywhere.
